Our Goal

As Britains first boutique sock shop we aim to provide high quality products without compromising on price. We also aim to provide a customer service that you wont anywhere else. We hope that every customer leaves with a once in a life time pair of socks

What We Do

All of our manufacturers go though intesive checks to make sure that all of their materials are ethically sourced. This process means that we can call ourselves the first fair trade sock shop in the world. We believe that everyone in the world has the right to a pair of socks so we have intiatives to make this a reality.
